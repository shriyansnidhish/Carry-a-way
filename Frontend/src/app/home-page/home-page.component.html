The easiest way to ship luggage

We can’t decide if the best part of using Carry-A-Way is the price or the convenience. And frankly, we’re not sure the answer even matters.

1. Book

We’ll find the cheapest FedEx or UPS rate and send a shipping label right to your phone.

2. Ship

Bring bags to a nearby carrier location. They can scan, print, and attach labels. Or, pay for a pickup.

3. Chill

Head to the airport with just the essentials and enjoy traveling without the worry to handle heavy loads.

4. Enjoy

Your luggage arrives at your final destination.
